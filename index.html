ASHFORD COUNTY SHERIFF // WITNESS INTERVIEW

Della Monroe, Housekeeper // Feb 14, 2026

Della Monroe, age 61, housekeeper for 23 years. Arrives 7:00 AM, departs 3:00 PM.

"I came in through the kitchen door. The kettle was already cold. I went to the sunroom. She was in her chair by the window. Cup on the table, half finished. I thought she was asleep. I said 'Good morning, Mrs. H' and she didn't move. I touched her shoulder and her skin was cold. I called 911."

Monroe confirmed David was present when she left at 3 PM on Feb 13. Russell was "in and out of the cottage." Unaware Lillian was expected.

"She was different. Quieter. She spent time in the greenhouse. Asked me to bring old boxes from the attic. Photo albums, letters. She seemed like she was getting her affairs in order."
